Corporate Partnerships with the American Chemical Society

Student Chapter at the University of Michigan

Our Mission:

The Student Members of ACS at the University of Michigan are dedicated to building strong partnerships with industry leaders to provide students with valuable opportunities for professional development and to explore potential careers in industry.

What We Offer:

We collaborate with corporate partners to host events such as recruitment sessions, company presentations, and career workshops. These events are designed to help students connect with potential employers and engage with potential future career paths.
